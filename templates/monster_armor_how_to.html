AP Level

AP Level stands for Armor Piercing level. It is a stat that describes what weapons can efeat the material of this armor. For more information, check out the combat 2.0 weapon rules in our github repo. Guidelines for level-appropriate Armor Piercing levels are listed below.

Movement Penalty

Heavier armors tend to have much better stats. To compensate, they are given a strength requirement known as a movement penalty. Please see the Combat 2.0 rules about mitigating movement speed for more information.

Please note that by convention, a positive number 2 in our form translates to a -2 meter per action movement penalty in play. Negative movement penalties would therefore translate to wearing armor that gives you a bonus to move. This is prevented by our system and will cause your armor to fail our validation check.
