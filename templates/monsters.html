Pirate Breacher

A 200lb 6' man holding a crude rusty shotgun walks with a heavy gait. His hair is greasy and wild, and should you get close enough, you smell that he clearly has not showered in days. He wears a gas mask over his face patched with duct tape, but the soft "cooh-pah" that it makes in time with his breathing clearly shows that it's functional.

Pirate Gunslinger

Never without his trusty cowboy hat or dual revolvers. Clearly shaves as though he has access to a razor, but doesn't really know how to use it right. Smells of cheap booze and few showers. Wears cowboy boots and a patched-up gas mask. Appears more attentive than most of his compatriots, and seems to keep his head on a swivel.

Cultist Initiate

A hooded figure wearing white robes seems to glide creepily along the ground. It's skin is a pale sickly green. As it waves its hands around, purple glowing nanite energy swirls in the air behind its gestures. Its movements are light, quick and deliberate. Despite the odds against it, it shows no signs of fear. Nor any emotion really at all.

Monster Bounty Board

Here's a short list of the monsters we still need to make and post. If you could put these together and email them to Mike, every DM would really appriciate it! Points are awarded based on how useful a given mob is likely to be in dungeon making and how complex the monster was to create. Points do not have any explicit value, but archDMs will honor a social contract and listen very closely to those from the community who are contributing. Custom monsters will have a score given to them upon website update. (until we find more programmers to automate the bounty system).

  • A level 1 pleb (DND goblin analogue) - 100 points
  • A level 3 Anti-armor unit - 70 points
  • A level 3 Pirate medic - 80 points
  • A level 10 standard pleb - 100 points
  • A level 5 showstopper for random encounters - 80 points
